Mission Impossible?



After the loss to Paris Saint-Germain in the first leg of the tie, Manchester United went on a good run of form by winning 3 and drawing 1 across their next 4 games. However, Manchester United heads into the second leg of the tie without key players such as Anthony Martial, Ander Herrera and Antonio Valencia due to injuries and Paul Pogba, the midfield maestro was suspended due to the incident from the first leg.



All of the pressure were on the remaining players to put up a good fight. And boy, what a good fight it was! Within the first 2 minutes of the game, Romelu Lukaku pounced on a stray pass from Thilo Kehrer to take the ball past PSG's captain, Thiago Silva, before rounding up the legendary Gianluigi Buffon to slot the ball into an empty net.





It feels like United actually had a chance. However, we were brought back down to earth after the next 10 minutes as Kylian Mbappe squared the ball across Manchester United's penalty box for Juan Bernat to slot into the net.





However, the warriors from Manchester refused to gave up. They pushed and pressed their opponents as hard as they could and in the 30th minute, Romelu Lukaku once again pounced on another mistake, this time by Gianluigi Buffon, who spilled the ball after a shot. Like the first goal, Romelu Lukaku made no mistake and placed the ball into the net.





Now the score was 2-1 and United need another goal to ensure that they move on to the next round of the competition. As the current results stand, PSG would be the one advancing to the next stage. As time went on, the possibility of a comeback happening was getting smaller and smaller. The injury-ravaged Manchester United had to count on their youths to change up the gameplan and on came Diogo Dalot, Tahith Chong and Mason Greenwood. The average age of the substitutes were 19!.





As fate would have it, on literally the last action of the game before the whistle would be blown, Diogo Dalot crossed a ball and it hit a PSG player on the way before going out for a corner. The United players went into a frenzy...



THEY WERE APPEALING FOR A HANDBALL! And the referee consulted the Video Assistant Referee for assistance as he did not witness the handball. The atmosphere was tense. There was dead silence at the Parc des Princes. Both home crowd and away crowd was waiting for the decision to be made. What was 30 seconds felt like an eternity.







THE DECISION WAS MADE! MANCHESTER UNITED HAVE BEEN AWARDED A PENALTY! Romelu Lukaku was on course for his hat trick but he passed the ball to 21 years old, Marcus Rashford, who have been a Manchester United player since he was 7, and told him that it was his time to shine. Up stood Marcus Rashford...

With a deep breath, he made his runup... The rest, as they say, is history.



MANCHESTER UNITED HAVE DONE IT ONCE AGAIN! Comesbacks are in the blood of the club and they have completed a historic comeback this time round. They are the first team to progress to the next stage of the Champions League after being down by 2 goals at home in the first leg. They have moved on to the Quarter Finals of the Champions League.
