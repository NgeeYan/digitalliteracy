Manchester United in Paris



It all started with Ángel Di María. The former Manchester United man was drawn against his former club in the 2018/19 UEFA Champions League's Round of 16 knockout stage. With the likes of world class stars such as Neymar and Mbappe and German tactician, Thomas Tuchel, at the helm, Paris Saint Germain was the heavy favourites going in into the tie. Unsurprisingly, Manchester United lost the first leg of the tie, 2-0, at their homeground. Going in into the second leg, can the boys mount a comeback and overturn a 2-0 deficit away from home that has never been done before? Fast forward 19 months, fate would have it that Manchester United will face off with PSG once again in the 2020/21 UEFA Champions League's Group Stages. Will the results once again be the same?
